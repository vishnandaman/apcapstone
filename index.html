What is Virtual Reality Therapy?

Virtual Reality Therapy integrates digital environments into therapeutic practices to enhance treatment for various mental health conditions. By engaging in simulated, interactive experiences, individuals can process emotions, practice coping techniques, and desensitize distressing stimuli in a safe and controlled way.

  • Military & Combat PTSD: Controlled exposure therapy

  • Fear of Flying: Pre-flight preparation sessions
  • Claustrophobia & Public Speaking: Repeated exposure practice
